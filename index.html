StrideScan

Take your sprinting to the next level.

Free, real-time form analysis. Just upload your sprinting video and gain insights to fine-tune your performance

Currently v1.2 · Built with Python, Ultralytics and Pytorch

A note from the creator

Hi, I'm Isaac, a high school senior.

This project began after I did some research on computer vision, specifically in the context of self-driving cars. Many courses, videos, and articles later, I realized that applying this technology to running could be extremely insightful.

I decided to take on the challenge myself. Being a sprinter on my school's track team, I know that form is important both for improving speed and preventing injury. And having a coach who always pointed out minor errors in my form gave me the foundational knowledge necessary to create this program.

Though this project ongoing, I believe it is a robust tool for improving running form, and demonstrates a viable proof of concept for digital coaches in other sports.
